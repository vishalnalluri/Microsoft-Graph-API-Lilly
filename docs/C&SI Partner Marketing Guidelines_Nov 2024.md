## Press releases 

## Share your successes with the world 

## **General guidance** 

To make the press release newsworthy, partners are encouraged to highlight a joint customer or to announce a new product/ service based on the Databricks platform, a joint Solution Accelerator or Brickbuilder Solution, new Partner Connect integration, etc. 

The press release should not be positioned or distributed as a joint press release; use of the Databricks boilerplate is not permitted.

## Press releases 

Share your successes with the world 

## **General guidance** 

Databricks must approve all press releases that mention the company, its executives and/or the Databricks Data Intelligence Platform. Allow at least 10 business days for Databricks' review cycle. 

Databricks reserves the right to alter and/or decline any proposed mentions or quotes per our guidelines.

## **Approval process** 

To make it easier for Databricks’ partners to develop press releases and streamline the review process, Databricks has developed new partner press release templates. **Please log in to the Partner Portal, download the template and make a copy of it before editing.** While we expect partners to personalize the release content based on their announcement and use case, the closer you keep the release to the template content, the faster we will be able to review it.
