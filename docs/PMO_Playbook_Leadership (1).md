## Slide 8

Skills Database & Hiring Decisions

Finding the right person --- and knowing when to hire externally

RADcube PMO Playbook  \|  Confidential

Skills Database --- How It Works

1

Baseline

HR provides employee resumes → PMO extracts skills

2

Tag in Zoho

Gowtham tags each employee: primary, secondary, domain, certs

3

Monthly Update

Team acquires new skill → HR notified → Zoho updated within 2 days

4

Audit

Rakesh audits directory completeness every month-end

When to Trigger an External Hire

Skill Gap

Required skill does not exist internally

Unavailability

Right person is fully booked for the required window

Overload

All relevant resources \>100% utilised for 2+ months

Strategic

New practice area needs dedicated headcount

Critical Failure Modes to Avoid

⚠  POs skip task creation

Dashboard shows no future capacity → planning becomes guesswork

⚠  Leaders bypass PMO

Direct resource invitations → conflicts, overload, invisible commitments

⚠  Stale Zoho data

Ghost employees or outdated skills → wrong allocation decisions

## Slide 10

KEY TAKEAWAYS

What Leadership Needs to Do

1

Always route through PMO

Every project --- P1 to P4 --- must go through the initiation process before team assignments.

2

Use the dashboard, not instinct

Staffing decisions must be backed by skill match + availability data from Zoho.

3

Keep Zoho current

No ghost employees. No missing skills. No untracked tasks. Garbage in = garbage out.

4

POs own their backlog

Without story-pointed, assigned tasks, capacity forecasting is impossible.
