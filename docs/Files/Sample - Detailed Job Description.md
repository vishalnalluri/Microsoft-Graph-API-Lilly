Detailed Job Description:  
 
The Programmer Analyst’s role is to analyze, define, develop, test, deploy and maintain 
software applications in support of the achievement of business need aligning to enterprise 
vision and strategy. This includes architectural evaluation, design and analysis of enterprise 
wide systems, coding, test plans, hardware/software assessment, deployment models and 
document technical architecture for software programs and applications throughout the 
software development life cycle.
